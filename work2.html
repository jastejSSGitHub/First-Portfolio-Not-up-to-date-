Overview

Being a chubby kid all my childhood, reaching my adoloscent years had me all up in fitness and health, where I had a hard time finding reliable and trustworthy information about workouts and especially nutrition. To help my fellow fitness lovers, I aimed to make an android application for a simple and easy to use fitness app with a decent database of exercises both for Building Muscle, Losing Weight and also following a proper diet which is essential for a fitness journey.

  • Features

    • Proper Nutrition Guide
    • BMI Calculator
    • GPS Locator

  • Challenges

    The biggest challenge I ecountered while developing this Java based platformer game, was the dynamics of the whole game, especially the main player's dynamics of him moving , jumping and interacting throughout the levels. In order to make the movement realistic, I could not just have a static image of a player while running left, right and jumping upwards. Therefore, I deviced a plan to animate the player.

  • Solution

    This was done by drawing a character idea from scratch, a muscular guy angry with the condition of his city. This was followed by scanning and rendering a character from all three sides (front , side and jumping version) in Adobe Illustrator. Then after rendering, I had each side of the character , imported into Adobe AfterEffects and divided its movement into 20 keyframes and saved it serially. Then used my newly learnt Java coding skills at that time to render all these keyframe images at a certain frames per second , to give the impression that the player is now dynamic and hence running and jumping on commands.

  • Final GamePlay

    Combining a dynamic animation of the main player, intelligently placed blocks and coins for a good playing challenge, and a mix of medium to hard enemies to beat and escape from, this game came out to life an interesting piece of a 2D platformer which would have me addicted at my video game playing prime.
