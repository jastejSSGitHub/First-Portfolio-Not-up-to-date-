Overview

Being a chubby kid all my childhood, reaching my adoloscent years had me all up in fitness and health, where I had a hard time finding reliable and trustworthy information about workouts and especially nutrition. To help my fellow fitness lovers, I aimed to make an android application for a simple and easy to use fitness app with a decent database of exercises both for Building Muscle, Losing Weight and also following a proper diet which is essential for a fitness journey.

  • Features

    • List of Exercises
    • Proper Nutrition Guide
    • BMI Calculator
    • GPS Locator

Process Analysis

FitLife is basically a fitness app for people both at intermediate and beginner level of fitness who want to make a change in their lives by being more active and having a better lifestyle.. I started with the ideation stage, where I brain dumped some layouts and came out to decide the following low fidelity sketches as my final layout of the application- A simple, minimalistic design for the user.

  • Basic Setup

    This entire environment was build in the Android Studio IDE, a super powerful tool for Java and C++programming using intelliJ, an amazing programming live help which helped me to cut short my learning curve while developing my first android application. This app startswith letting the user make a valid account and then giving them options to either select workout goals and follow respective diets and also a calculator to calculate how many calories one should consume. Another feature of the app is to get to know about oneself through calculating your Body Mass Index. Further, users can also take progress photos and save it on their devices as well as track their exact locations in order to check the areas around them.

  • Final App Run

    Combining a minimalistic design of the app structure, intelligently designed workout and nutrition plans, a BMI Calculator for keeping track of weight , and a mix of Step tracker and GPS Tracker, this app was build to cater to the intermediate fitness lovers who want a light and easy to use fitness app to maintain their lifestyles.
